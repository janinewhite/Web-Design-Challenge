About MASS SHOOTINGS IN AMERICA

This data set was compiled from online reports beginning in 2012 and includes mass shootings from 1966 though June 2016.

In this dataset, a mass shooting is an incident with 3 or more victims, not including the shooter, that is not identifiably rerlated gang, drug, or organized crime. 
Only mass shootings in America are available in this dataset. 

 The data may not be comparable to other mass shooting datasets, depending on how they were collected and the definition of mass shooting used to compile them.  Other studies have used different criteria for inclusion, for example, they may have chosen a different number of victims required to define a mass shooting and differentiatied by outcome or types of incidents captured.
