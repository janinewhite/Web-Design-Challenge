About MASS SHOOTINGS IN AMERICA

This data set was compiled from online reports beginning in 2012 and includes mass shootings from 1966 though June 2016.

A mass shooting is an incident with 3 or more victims, not including the shooter, that is not identifiably rerlated gang, drug, or organized crime.

The original data is available from the MSA Github repository.
